How to Play Nonogram

  1. Look at the numbers: These numbers show how many squares to fill in each row and column.
  2. Fill in squares: Based on the numbers, fill in squares on the grid. If you see '3', it means fill in 3 squares together.
  3. Leave spaces: If you see '3 2', fill in 3 squares together, leave at least one square blank, then fill in 2 squares together.
  4. Use deduction: As you fill in squares, you can figure out where other squares should be filled.
  5. Complete the picture: Keep filling in squares until you reveal a picture!

Remember, the numbers show groups of filled squares. Each group is separated by at least one blank square.
